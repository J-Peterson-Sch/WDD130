White Water Rafting Co.

At White Water Rafting Co., our mission is to inspire adventure and foster a deep connection with nature through unforgettable river experiences. We are dedicated to providing safe, thrilling, and inclusive white water rafting adventures that bring people together, challenge limits, and create lifelong memories.

History

White Water Rafting Co. was founded over 25 years ago by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who shared a passion for rivers and a love of adventure. Starting with just a few rafts and a dream, the founders dedicated themselves to introducing people to the thrill and beauty of white water rafting.

Our first trips were small, operating on a single river, but word quickly spread about their expertise, friendly guides, and unforgettable experiences. As the company grew, so did its offerings, expanding to include guided adventures on some of the most iconic rivers in the country.
