Join Us On One of Our Amazing Trips

Elves Chasm
A trip to Elves Chasm with White Water Rafting Co. is nothing short of magical. Nestled within the heart of the Grand Canyon, this hidden oasis offers a breathtaking blend of adventure and serenity. Paddle through exhilarating rapids before arriving at the enchanting waterfall, where emerald pools and lush greenery create a surreal escape from the world. The journey to Elves Chasm is a once-in-a-lifetime experience, combining the thrill of white water rafting with the awe of discovering one of nature’s most beautiful treasures.

Snohomish River
A white water rafting trip down the Snohomish River is an exhilarating journey through the heart of Washington’s stunning natural beauty. With its dynamic mix of thrilling rapids and calm stretches, this river offers the perfect balance of adventure and relaxation. As you navigate the currents, you’ll be surrounded by towering evergreens, wildlife, and breathtaking mountain views. Whether you're conquering the rapids or enjoying the peaceful flow of the water, every moment on the Snohomish River is filled with excitement, making it an unforgettable experience for adventurers of all skill levels.

Southern Utah
A white water rafting trip through the canyons of Southern Utah is an adventure like no other, immersing you in a landscape of towering red rock walls, hidden waterfalls, and sunlit sandstone formations. As you navigate thrilling rapids and tranquil stretches of river, you’ll feel the excitement of discovery at every turn. Each twist in the canyon reveals stunning vistas, ancient geological wonders, and a sense of awe that only nature can inspire. Whether you’re chasing adrenaline or soaking in the serenity of the Southwest, this journey is an unforgettable blend of adventure and beauty.
